Welcome to the 8th Symposium

We are pleased to announce the 8th Symposium on Biophysics Postgraduate Research in Hong Kong, to be held on January 11, 2026 at the HKUST campus.

This one-day symposium provides a dynamic platform for postgraduate students, faculty, and researchers working across biophysics, physics, chemistry, and biology to present their work and foster interdisciplinary collaborations.

The event will feature keynote lectures by internationally recognized scientists, invited talks from leading institutions, and a poster session with awards for the best student contributions.

Quick Details

  • Date: January 11, 2026
  • Venue: The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)

Contact Email: shihong.chen@connect.ust.hk

This event is supported by the Society of Interdisciplinary Research and hosted by HKUST.
